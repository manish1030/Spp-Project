About Us

Established as a Sole Proprietorship firm in the year 2005, we “Sun Power Photovoltaic” are a leading Manufacturer and Trader of a wide range of Solar Light, Solar Panel, Solar Inverter, Solar Tubular Battery, etc. Situated in Ghaziabad (Uttar Pradesh, India), we have constructed a wide and well functional infrastructural unit that plays an important role in the growth of our company.
